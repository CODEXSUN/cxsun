# Purchase Entry

Use Purchase Entry to record supplier bills. Purchase records help track supplier amounts, input tax, stock or expense value, and payment status.

![Purchase flow](/img/docs/purchase-flow.svg)

## Before You Start

- Confirm company and accounting year.
- Create supplier contact if missing.
- Create products with unit, HSN, and tax.
- Keep supplier bill number and bill date ready.

## Create A Purchase

1. Open Billing > Purchase.
2. Select the supplier.
3. Enter supplier bill number and supplier bill date.
4. Confirm entry number and entry date.
5. Select Purchase Ledger when required.
6. Select purchase tax type: CGST-SGST or IGST.
7. Add product lines with quantity, rate, unit, HSN, and tax.
8. Review totals and save.

## When To Use Purchase Ledger

Use Purchase Ledger to classify the bill, such as normal purchase, fabric purchase, garment purchase, accessories purchase, or any account category your office uses.

## Checks Before Saving

- Supplier name is correct.
- Supplier bill number matches the original bill.
- Bill date is correct.
- Tax type matches the supplier state and bill.
- Product quantity and rate match the bill.
- GST total matches the supplier bill.

## Reporting

Supplier statement reports help compare purchase bills and payments.
