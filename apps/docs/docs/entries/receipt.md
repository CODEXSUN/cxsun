# Receipt Entry

Use Receipt Entry when money is received from a customer or another party.

![New Receipt entry screen](/img/docs/entries/receipt-entry.png)

## Before You Start

- Create the customer or party.
- Confirm the cash or bank ledger that receives the money.
- Keep the bank, UPI, cheque, or other reference ready.
- Identify the Sales invoices to allocate.

## Create a Receipt

1. Open **Billing > Entries > Receipts**.
2. Select **New Receipt**.
3. Search and select the **Customer name**.
4. Confirm the **Receipt no** and **Date**.
5. Enter the total **Amount** received.
6. Select the payment **Mode**.
7. Select the receiving **Cash ledger** or **Bank ledger**.
8. Enter the **Work Order no** and notes when applicable.

## Allocate the Receipt

1. Open the **Allocations** tab.
2. Select each open Sales invoice being paid.
3. Enter the amount allocated to each invoice.
4. Confirm that total allocation does not exceed the receipt amount.
5. Leave the balance unallocated only for an advance or on-account receipt.

## Save and Verify

1. Select **Save**.
2. Reopen the receipt and confirm customer, amount, mode, ledger, and allocation.
3. Check the Customer Statement to confirm the balance changed correctly.
4. Print or share the receipt when required.

## Common Mistakes

- Selecting Payment instead of Receipt for incoming money.
- Selecting the wrong cash or bank ledger.
- Allocating more than the receipt amount.
- Leaving an invoice unpaid because the receipt was saved without allocation.
