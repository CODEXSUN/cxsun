# Receipts And Payments

Receipts record money received from customers. Payments record money paid to suppliers or other parties.

![Money entry flow](/img/docs/money-entry-flow.svg)

## Receipts

1. Open Billing > Receipts.
2. Select customer or party.
3. Enter receipt date, amount, mode, and reference.
4. Allocate against open invoices where available.
5. Save the receipt.

Use Receipt when money comes in by cash, bank, UPI, cheque, or another accepted mode.

## Payments

1. Open Billing > Payments.
2. Select supplier or party.
3. Enter payment date, amount, mode, and reference.
4. Allocate against open bills where available.
5. Save the payment.

Use Payment when money goes out to suppliers or other parties.

## Allocation

Allocation connects a receipt to invoices or a payment to bills. When allocation is available, select the open document and enter the amount applied to it. This keeps statements clear.

## Check After Saving

- Amount is correct.
- Cash or bank mode is correct.
- Reference number is entered when needed.
- Statement balance changed as expected.

## Review

Use customer and supplier statements to verify balances after posting receipts or payments.
