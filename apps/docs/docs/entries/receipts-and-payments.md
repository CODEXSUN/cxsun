# Receipts And Payments

Receipts record money received from customers. Payments record money paid to suppliers or other parties.

## Receipts

1. Open Billing > Receipts.
2. Select customer or party.
3. Enter receipt date, amount, mode, and reference.
4. Save the receipt.

## Payments

1. Open Billing > Payments.
2. Select supplier or party.
3. Enter payment date, amount, mode, and reference.
4. Save the payment.

## Review

Use customer and supplier statements to verify balances after posting receipts or payments.
