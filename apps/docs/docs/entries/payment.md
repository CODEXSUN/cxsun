# Payment Entry

Use Payment Entry when money is paid to a supplier or another party.

![New Payment entry screen](/img/docs/entries/payment-entry.png)

## Before You Start

- Create the supplier or party.
- Confirm the cash or bank ledger used for payment.
- Keep cheque, bank, UPI, or other reference details ready.
- Identify the Purchase bills to allocate.

## Create a Payment

1. Open **Billing > Entries > Payments**.
2. Select **New Payment**.
3. Search and select the **Supplier name**.
4. Confirm the **Payment no** and **Date**.
5. Enter the total **Amount** paid.
6. Select the payment **Mode**.
7. Select the paying **Cash ledger** or **Bank ledger**.
8. Enter the **Work Order no** and notes when applicable.

## Allocate the Payment

1. Open the **Allocations** tab.
2. Select each open Purchase bill being paid.
3. Enter the amount allocated to each bill.
4. Confirm that total allocation does not exceed the payment amount.
5. Leave a balance unallocated only when recording an advance or on-account payment.

## Save and Verify

1. Select **Save**.
2. Reopen the payment and confirm supplier, amount, mode, ledger, and allocation.
3. Check the Supplier Statement to verify the outstanding balance.
4. Print or share the payment voucher when required.

## Common Mistakes

- Selecting Receipt instead of Payment for outgoing money.
- Paying from the wrong cash or bank ledger.
- Allocating the payment to the wrong supplier bill.
- Recording bank charges inside the supplier payment without the correct ledger treatment.
