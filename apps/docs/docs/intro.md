# Codexsun User Guide

Codexsun is a daily business workspace for billing, accounts-ready entries, master records, reports, and document control. It keeps repeated work organized so your team can create records once and reuse them everywhere.

![Codexsun work flow](/img/docs/codexsun-workflow.svg)

## What Is Included

- **Workspace access:** login, company selection, accounting year, and dashboard navigation.
- **Common records:** reusable values such as units, taxes, HSN codes, locations, terms, and product groups.
- **Master records:** company profile, customers, suppliers, products, and work orders.
- **Billing entries:** quotations, sales invoices, purchase bills, receipts, and payments.
- **Reports:** customer statements, quotation status, monthly summaries, and printable views.
- **Settings:** document numbering, print details, company defaults, and feature controls.

## How This Helps

- Reduces repeated typing by reusing saved contacts, products, taxes, units, and addresses.
- Keeps document numbers and print details consistent.
- Helps staff enter bills and receipts in the same pattern every day.
- Makes reports easier to check because entries, payments, and balances are connected.
- Gives owners and office teams a clear path from setup to daily work to review.

## Daily Flow

Most work follows this simple path:

1. Confirm company and accounting year.
2. Update common records if a new tax, unit, location, or term is needed.
3. Create or update customers, suppliers, and products.
4. Enter quotation, sale, purchase, receipt, or payment.
5. Print, download, or share the document.
6. Check reports for pending amounts and follow-up.
