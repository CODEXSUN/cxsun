# Customer Statement

Customer Statement shows customer billing, receipts, and balance movement.

![Statement review](/img/docs/statement-review.svg)

## Use It To

- Check pending invoices.
- Compare sales and receipts.
- Share account summaries with customers.
- Review balances for follow-up.

## Steps

1. Open Billing > Report > Customer Statement.
2. Select date range.
3. Select customer.
4. Review opening balance.
5. Check sales and receipt rows.
6. Review closing balance.
7. Print or download if needed.

## What To Check

- Old pending invoices.
- Receipts not allocated to invoices.
- Balance mismatch with customer records.
- Date range used for review.

## Best Use

Use this report before payment follow-up and after entering receipts.
