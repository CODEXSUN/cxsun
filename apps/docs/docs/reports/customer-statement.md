# Customer Statement

Customer Statement shows customer billing, receipts, and balance movement.

## Use It To

- Check pending invoices.
- Compare sales and receipts.
- Share account summaries with customers.
- Review balances for follow-up.

## Steps

1. Open Billing > Report > Customer Statement.
2. Select date range.
3. Select customer if needed.
4. Review opening, transaction, and closing details.
