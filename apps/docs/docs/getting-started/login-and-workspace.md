# Login And Workspace

Use the Codexsun login link and credentials given by your office administrator. Login opens the workspace where your company work is handled.

![Login and workspace flow](/img/docs/login-workspace.svg)

## Login

1. Open your Codexsun workspace URL.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Click Login.
4. Confirm that the correct company name appears in the sidebar.
5. Confirm the accounting year before creating entries.

## First Check After Login

- Company name is correct.
- Accounting year is correct.
- Side menu shows the apps you need.
- Your name or logout option is visible in the top area.

## If Something Looks Wrong

- Do not create a new invoice until company and accounting year are correct.
- Refresh the page once if the dashboard looks empty.
- Ask your administrator if a menu is missing.
- Logout and login again if another user was using the same computer.

## Logout

Use the Logout button from the top bar when you finish work on a shared computer.
