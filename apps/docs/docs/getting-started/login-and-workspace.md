# Login And Workspace

Use your Codexsun login link and credentials from your administrator.

## Login

1. Open your Codexsun workspace URL.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Select the correct workspace if your account has access to more than one tenant.
4. Confirm that the correct company appears in the sidebar before creating entries.

## Workspace Selection

The workspace controls which tenant database you are using. If you switch workspace, contacts, products, entries, reports, and settings change to that tenant.

## Logout

Use the Logout button from the top bar when you finish work on a shared computer.
