# Company And Master Data

Before daily billing work, set up your company, contacts, products, and common master data.

## Company

Keep company legal name, GSTIN, address, bank, logo, and contact details updated. These details are used in prints and reports.

## Contacts

Create customer and supplier contacts before making quotations, sales, purchases, receipts, or payments.

## Products

Create products with unit, HSN, tax, and category details so entries can calculate cleanly.

## Common Master Data

Use common setup pages for countries, states, cities, payment terms, units, taxes, brands, warehouses, and other reusable options.
