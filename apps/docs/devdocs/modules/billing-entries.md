# Billing Entries Modules

Billing entries cover quotation, sales, export sales, purchase, receipt, and payment workflows.

## Modules

- Quotation Entry.
- Sales Entry.
- Export Sales Entry.
- Purchase Entry.
- Receipt Entry.
- Payment Entry.

## Responsibilities

- Capture tenant-local business transactions.
- Store line items, comments, and activity records where supported.
- Use company, contact, product, common setup, and document settings.
